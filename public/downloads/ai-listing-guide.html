Chapter 5: Best Practices

Before You Generate

Walk through the property (physically or virtually)
Note unique features and recent updates
Identify target buyer profile
Know the comparable sales

What to Always Include

  • Updated features (kitchen, baths, etc.)
  • Outdoor spaces and views
  • Storage and parking
  • Neighborhood highlights
  • School information (for family homes)

After You Generate

Read all 3 variations
Pick your favorite as starting point
Add personal touches and local knowledge
Verify all facts for accuracy
Adjust for platform requirements (MLS limits, etc.)
