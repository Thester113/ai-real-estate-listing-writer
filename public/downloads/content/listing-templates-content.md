The primary suite offers a true retreat with [SPECIAL FEATURE: spa-like bathroom, private balcony, fireplace, etc.]. Additional bedrooms provide generous proportions, each with [FEATURE: en-suite bath, walk-in closet, etc.].

Step outside to discover [OUTDOOR FEATURE: manicured grounds, infinity pool, outdoor kitchen, etc.]. Located in the prestigious [NEIGHBORHOOD], this residence offers proximity to [NEARBY AMENITY: top-rated schools, fine dining, country club, etc.].

# General Customization Tips

## The First Sentence Rule
Your first sentence must grab attention. Use one of these patterns:

1. **Location Lead**: "In the heart of [DESIRABLE AREA]..."
2. **Feature Lead**: "With [STANDOUT FEATURE]..."
3. **Lifestyle Lead**: "For those who [DESIRE]..."
4. **Urgency Lead**: "Rarely does a home like this..."

## Words to Avoid
- ~~Motivated seller~~ (signals desperation)
- ~~Cozy~~ for large spaces (use for small spaces only)
- ~~Unique~~ without explanation (too vague)
- ~~Must see~~ (overused and empty)

## Length Guidelines
- **MLS Description**: 250-500 words
- **Highlight/Teaser**: 50-100 words
- **Social Media**: 50-150 words + hashtags
